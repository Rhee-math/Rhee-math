Concepts
Number theory
Modular arithmetic
The modulus function is a very important topic in number theory that is used from competition to olympiad level questions. In this lessons, we explain the modulus function and fermat's little theorem, a commonly used equation.
Factors & Divisors
Factors and divisors are extremely important in all parts of algebra as they allow you to find the roots of equations, the properties of certain numbers, and the ability to solve complex number problems. In this lesson, we delve into the technical side behind factors and divisors.
Sequence & Series
Sequences and series are extremely important patterns of numbers. These concepts are the root of fractals and can even go on to infinity. These "objects" are extremely interesting and are crucial to know.
Counting and probability
Combinations & Permutations
Combinations and permutations are the foundation of all of counting and probability. With a large portion of competition math questions being about counting and probability, knowing these devices will be paramount to both your understanding and score. In this lesson, we will explain these devices and their application.
Binomial theorem
The binomial theorem is one of the founding ideas in all of counting and probability. This theorem utilizes pascals triangle. In this lesson, the applications and the abilities of this theorem will be shared, giving you the tools to solve more probability and counting problems!
Stars & Bars
Stars and bars has a fun name and a fun application! This concept is extremely useful in solving distribution problems and has wide applications over multiple problems. In this lesson, we will show you the ins and outs of this technique and problems involving the technique.
Geometry
Intro to Geometry
Geometry is a very complex branch of mathematics with a strong foundation being required to be successful. In this lesson, we build that foundation for you, delving into topics such as 3d shapes and basic geometric rules.
Triangular geometry
Triangular geometry is the most important aspect of geometry in competition math, especially in the earlier stages such as AMC, as the majority of geometry questions will require some level of knowledge on triangular geometry. In this lesson, we show the surface level of this type of geometry.
Power of a point
The concept of "power of a point" is one concerning angles inside of circles. Angles inside of circles are a large part of geometry, especially in AMC geometry questions. In this lesson, we show the applications of this important concept and provide you with the tools to find angles in circles yourself.
