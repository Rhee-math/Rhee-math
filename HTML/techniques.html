Strategies for the AMC test
The AMC is one of, if not the most popular competition for highschool mathematics. Not only is it important to know the concepts that you must understand to succeed, but it is important to know which questions to answer and which questions to skip, along with overall test strategy. This portion gives insight into this problem, giving students the ability to be successful on test day.
Bashing
On certain questions in the AMC, bashing may be an extremely useful strategy. When you are feeling stuck on a counting problem, you feel the urge to find every single possibility so that you can solve the problem. Just like this example, we teach you how to utilize the art of bashing in competition math to be more successful.
