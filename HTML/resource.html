Resources
GLeaM is an amazing resource for the AMC and other competitions as they provide multiple resources and lessons on important subjects. They give sample problems and provide you with important knowledge.
Richard Rusczyk is one of the most reliable and notable names in competition math. He is the CEO of AOPS and has multiple resources regarding problem solving techniques and hard problems. His content is very accessible and very helpful for all students of different levels.
The AOPS books are extremely useful for beginner to intermediate problem solvers. You can learn thouroughly about specific topics such as algebra and counting & probability, or you can cover a large amount of knowledge with their "volume" books.
Sohil Rathi is a high school student who has created three different books for competition math. Omega learn is an educational website with many resources for you to prepare with. They also provide free classes and give multiple learning opportunities which a student can use!
Alcumus is an extension of the AOPS organization, which can be seen as a problem generating website. You will be able to solve a variety of problems using alcumus and will able to apply the knowledge you have learnt in a realistic scenario, making this the perfect resource for beginner and intermediate problem solvers.
