About Us

Rhee-Math is an educational website geared towards teaching and improving upon the mathematical skillset of students. Rhee-Math challenges students to stretch their creativity and through comprehensive lessons, is able to prepare students for competitions such as the AMC. Rhee-Math is a non-profit organization and our mission statement is to "make mathematics more enjoyable and accessible for all". Rhee-Math is founded and led by students, and has multiple lessons from the perspective of prospective students who have been involved in competition math.

What is competition math?

Competition math is a complex and extremely interesting world. This world is comprised, as the name suggests, of competitions which give students challenging questions to either solve or prove. There are many different competitions available to students internationally, and students must not only think critically but must also have extensive knowledge on different aspects of mathematics. The majority of competition math happens at the high school level, such as AMC 10/12 and UKMT competitions. Our website works to make these competitions more accessible and to help students succeed in these competitions, giving them more insight and interest into the world of mathematics and the tools to use mathematics in all of their pursuits.
